Introduction

Hello!

I recently graduated from the University of Wisconsin, Madison with double majors in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in Spring 2020. In these trying times, I am thankful to be offered a position as a Software Engineer and am currently working at Electronic Arts.

I relish in the process of tackling hard problems and finding the best solution possible. Learning about software development in classes was never enough for me as I sought to apply my knowledge on real world problems. Coupling an insatiable curiosity with an aptitude for picking up new skills quickly, I have a wide array of experiences from back end development to cross platform mobile and desktop app development.

On my own time, I am currently working on a chatbot hosted on telegram. My grand vision for it would be to train it to the point where it can reliably imitate my speech patterns. Hopefully, it will be able to pass the Turing Test with my friends and family!!
